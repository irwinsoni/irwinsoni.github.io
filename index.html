StudyTimer

Put A Stop To Procastination And Get StudyTimer

StudyTimer is a productivity app which helps users in focusing on
study/work by locking selected apps on their phone for a
given time period.

  • Select From Apps On Your Phone
  • Check Apps You Want To Lock
  • Put A Timer On Checked Apps
  • Your Apps Are Locked
  • Get The Most Out Of Your Time

Select From Apps On Your Phone

By using StudyTimer, you can block distractions on your phone and get the most out of your study/work time. StudyTimer lets you choose from a list of all the applications in your phone

Check Apps You Want To Lock

From the list of all you phone apps, you can check selected apps you want to lock.

Put A Timer On Checked Apps

After checking the apps, you can select the time you want to lock them for.

Your Apps Are Locked

Your selected apps are locked now and you can open them using a pattern/pin code.

Get The Most Out Of Your Time

It's time to put your concentration at the right place and get lots of work done.
